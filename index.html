ABOUT US

What's Run For Hope?

Run For Hope (RFH) is an annual run organised by Four Seasons Hotel Singapore and the National Cancer Centre Singapore (NCCS) to raise much-needed awareness and support for cancer research. For more than 30 years, the Four Seasons group of hotel & resorts across the world have been deeply committed to the cause of advancing cancer research.

Our Role

In Singapore, we believe that our mission is to plan, seucre sponsorship and raise awareness for the run, in order to give them back to the community. We're always willing to actively promote and donate to the cancer causes.

Why Run For Hope?

Joining us to support the run and continue our legacy, you will have the opportunities to:

  • Support for cancer patients and their families
  • Bust off daily work stress
  • Keep fit, stay healthy!
  • Have fun and make more friends

Each year, more than 10,000 people are diagnosed with cancer. The threat of cancer isvery real; however, with advanced in science and robust cancer research activities, we are hopeful that the progressive breakthroughs made by us can lead to a cure. Fund raised will be donated to NCC Research Fund.

Through your support, we can make a difference in the lives of those affected. LET'S MAKE A BRIGHTER FUTURE, WITHOUR CANCER!!
